The situation

You've been solving the wrong problem.

You've spent real time trying to move your life forward. Reading, planning, thinking about what needs to change. And then you go home and do the same thing you did yesterday. And the day before that. And the six months before that.

The hours spent numbing out after work. The arguments you rehearse with people who aren't in the room. The coping mechanisms you've never once questioned because they're just "what you do." The relationships where nothing changes but you keep showing up the same way expecting different results.

You're not lazy. You're not undisciplined. You're spending an enormous amount of energy every day. It's just pre-allocated to things you've never looked at.

That's the actual problem. Not a lack of capability. A lack of visibility into where the capability is already going.
