How This Works

This assessment measures 7 dimensions of personal effectiveness through 28 questions. It takes about 10 minutes.

You'll encounter different question types — image selections, quick-tap pairs, scenarios, sliders, and more. There are no right or wrong answers. Answer based on how you actually are, not how you want to be.

At the end, you'll receive a rank (1-7) for each dimension and an overall effectiveness profile. Enter your email to access your results.

Your responses are confidential and used only to generate your profile.
